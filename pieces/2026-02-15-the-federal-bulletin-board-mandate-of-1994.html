Standards For Electronic Bulletin Boards
Required Under Part 284 of the Commission's Regulations
Issued: January 5, 1994
Each interstate pipeline shall maintain
an electronic bulletin board
accessible by personal computer and modem
The following information shall be posted:
— Available capacity
— Scheduled volume
— Operational data
— Receipt and delivery point information
— Index of customers
